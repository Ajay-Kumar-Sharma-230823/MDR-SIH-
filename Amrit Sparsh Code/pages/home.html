About Amrit Sparsh

The End of Blind Spots in Infection Control.

Traditional contact tracing is slow; bacteria are fast. Amrit Sparsh closes the gap. We don't just collect data; we turn your hospital into a smart facility that "sees" infection risks in real-time.

Powered by advanced intelligence, Amrit Sparsh tracks the complex web of patient movements and MDR carriers automatically. No more manual logs, no more delayed responses. We give your doctors and administrators the one thing they need most: Time. Time to isolate, time to contain, and time to save lives.
